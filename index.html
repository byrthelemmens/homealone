Home Alone

Home Alone is a residency program for artists, concluding with an event that will evolve around both the work made and the making, from and for people inside and outside of the arts.

Home Alone at the Vondelstraat

Between 26.05.15 — 14.06.15, Home Alone will take place in Amsterdam, at the house of Kater’s parents, while they are on a holiday.

Working Period 1

Petros Panagiotis Orfanos in collaboration with Lotte Boon will work at Home Alone from 26.05.15 — 30.05.15, concluding their working period with an event taking place on 31.05.15.
