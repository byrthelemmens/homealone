Home Alone

Home Alone is a residency program for artists consisting of a working period of 5 days, concluding with a one day event. This event will evolve around both the work made and the making, from and for people inside and outside of the arts.

Home Alone at the Vondelstraat

Between May 26 and June 14 Home Alone will take place in Amsterdam, at the house of the parents of initiator Tirza Kater, while they are on a holiday.
